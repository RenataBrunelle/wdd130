The purpose of Rafting Adventure is to provide unique adventure experiences and connection with nature through rafting, with the mission of offering safe, exciting and sustainable rafting activities, promoting ecological tourism and the well-being of participants. History

Rafting Adventure was born in 2012, when a group of friends passionate about extreme sports decided to turn their passion for rapids into a life project. Located on the banks of the Rio das Pedras, the company started with just two boats and a great desire to show the public the beauty and strength of nature. Over time, professionalism, care for safety and environmental commitment made Rafting Adventure a reference in adventure tourism in the region. Today, the company offers complete experiences that go beyond rafting, integrating ecotourism, environmental education and experiences of personal improvement, always with the team spirit and emotion that define its essence.
